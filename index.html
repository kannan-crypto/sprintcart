FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with sprintcart.

What industries does sprintcart serve?
+
sprintcart is purpose-built for the BFSI sector — serving banks, mutual funds, insurance companies, and NBFCs with large, distributed channel networks. Our solutions are also adaptable for enterprises in other sectors that manage nationwide distribution and partner engagement at scale.
Are the 4 products sold together or can we subscribe individually?
+
Each of sprintcart's four solutions — Genius Card, DigiSafe, Supply Junction, and Jubilee — can be deployed independently based on your business need. Many clients start with one product and expand into the full ecosystem as they scale. Together, they form an integrated engagement and operations platform.
How long does onboarding typically take?
+
Onboarding timelines vary by product and organization size. Genius Card can be deployed within days for individual teams. DigiSafe and Jubilee typically go live in 2–4 weeks. Supply Junction, given its procurement workflow integrations, may take 4–8 weeks for full enterprise rollout. Our team handles end-to-end onboarding.
Is sprintcart compliant with BFSI data security regulations?
+
Yes. sprintcart is ISO/IEC 27001:2022 certified, which is the international benchmark for information security management. The platform includes role-based access controls, complete audit logs, encrypted data storage and transit, and is designed to align with RBI and IRDAI data governance guidelines.
Does sprintcart support regional languages?
+
Yes. DigiSafe includes DigiBhasha — an AI-powered multilingual content engine that automatically translates and adapts marketing materials into regional languages. This is especially valuable for BFSI enterprises reaching channel partners and customers across diverse linguistic geographies in India.
What is the minimum organization size to use sprintcart?
+
sprintcart is built for mid-to-large enterprises, typically those with 100+ employees or 50+ channel partners in their network. Supply Junction, for instance, is designed to serve indent requests from 100 to 50,000 employees. If you're unsure whether it's the right fit, book a demo and our team will assess your needs honestly.
Does sprintcart offer a free trial or proof of concept?
+
We offer a structured product demo and, for qualifying enterprises, a scoped proof-of-concept engagement. This helps both parties evaluate the fit before a full deployment. Book a demo to start the conversation — our team will walk you through the relevant product in your context.
